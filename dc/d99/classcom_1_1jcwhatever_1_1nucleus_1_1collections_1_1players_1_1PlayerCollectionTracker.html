Detailed Description

Tracks a collection of player objects or player representative objects and aids in automatically removing the player entry when the player logs out.

The player collection is responsible for calling notifyPlayerRemoved method when a player is removed from the collection and notifyPlayerAdded when a player is added to the collection.
