void showTo ( Player  player)

Show the action bar to a player.

Displays using ActionBarPriority#DEFAULT.

If the player is viewing 1 or more IPersistentActionBar's, a ITimedActionBar is shown instead to ensure the text is given a proper time slice among the persisted bars.
