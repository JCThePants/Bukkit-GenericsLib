boolean removePlayer ( Player  player,
boolean  force 
)

Remove a player from the playlist.

Unless forced or the player is in a different world, the players sound queue is marked for removal and removed when the currently playing sound ends.

Forcing the immediate removal of the players sound queue does not end the sound on the client.

Parameters
playerThe player to remove.
forceTrue to force the immediate removal of the players sound queue.
